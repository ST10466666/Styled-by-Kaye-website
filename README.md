## Project Overview
Styled By Kaye is a small business nail service based in Johannesburg, Gauteng, established in 2024. The business provides professional nail services including gel nails, acrylic nails, manicures, pedicures, and custom nail art. The website showcases services, gallery of work, and allows clients to make enquiries or book appointments online.

---

## Project Goals
- Build a visually appealing and professional website.  
- Showcase nail services with images of completed work.  
- Provide clients with information on services, pricing, and contact details.  
- Allow clients to submit enquiries or book appointments online.  
- Promote the brand online with SEO-friendly content and maintain accessibility.

---

## Project Structure & Parts

### Part 1 – HTML Structure
- Created semantic HTML structure for all pages (`index.html`, `about.html`, `services.html`, `gallery.html`, `enquiry.html`, `contact.html`)  
- Proper use of `<header>`, `<main>`, `<footer>`, `<section>`, `<article>` for accessibility  
- Navigation menu included on all pages  
- Forms for enquiries and contact information

---

### Part 2 – CSS Styling & Responsive Design
- External stylesheet (`style.css`) linked to all pages  
- **Color Scheme:** Soft pink (#ffb6c1) with accent colors (#ff6f91, #ffe4e9)  
- **Typography:** Poppins font for a modern and clean appearance  
- Hero section with background image  
- Service and gallery cards with hover and zoom animations  
- Enquiry and contact forms styled as full-page professional forms  
- Fully responsive layout using Flexbox and Grid  
- Buttons, navigation, and cards with hover effects and transitions

---

### Part 3 – JavaScript & Interactivity
- **Lightbox gallery** for services: click an image to zoom and navigate to next/previous image  
- Form submission feedback messages (e.g., "We have received your enquiry")  
- Mobile-friendly toggle navigation
